# Growth Stock Checklist

## User question

> I want to screen a growth stock without relying on hype. What should I verify?

## Recommended skill

```text
biga-growth-skill/SKILL.md
```

## Example output

```markdown
# Growth Stock Research Checklist

## 1. Growth source

- Does growth come from real demand, market share gains, new products, or price increases?
- Is revenue growth supported by orders, deliveries, users, or usage data?
- Is the company relying on subsidies, aggressive discounts, or acquisitions?

## 2. Growth quality

- Does net profit growth match revenue growth?
- Is operating cash flow improving with earnings?
- Are receivables, inventory, or contract assets growing faster than revenue?

## 3. Competitive position

- Does the company have durable product, cost, technology, channel, or brand advantages?
- Is competition intensifying or moving toward price wars?
- Are customers concentrated in a few large accounts?

## 4. Valuation and expectation

- What growth rate is already implied by the current valuation?
- What happens if growth slows earlier than expected?
- Is the stock priced for a best-case scenario?

## 5. Risk triggers

- Revenue growth slows while valuation remains high.
- Cash flow diverges from reported profit.
- Industry supply increases faster than demand.
- Management relies heavily on financing or acquisitions.

> This is for investment research and education only. It is not investment advice.
```

## Boundary note

This example avoids stock picking, price targets, trading signals, and return guarantees.
