What is Biga?

Biga is a Markdown-native Skill collection for AI Agents. It helps Agents produce structured research logic, verification checklists, risk boundaries, and follow-up questions instead of direct trading conclusions. 

git clone https://github.com/sun-btc/biga.git

Features

Six strategies

Value, growth, trend, dividend, cyclical, and event-driven research.

Markdown-native

No runtime dependency. Easy to read, edit, copy, and integrate.

Agent-friendly

Works with Claude, Comate, Cursor, and other Markdown-aware Agents.

Risk-first

Explicit boundaries, verification checklists, and non-advice disclaimers.

Strategy Skills

Value

Fundamentals, cash flow, valuation, value traps.

Growth

Growth stocks, AI, semiconductors, new energy, innovation themes.

Trend

Moving averages, breakouts, pullbacks, and stop-loss discipline.

Dividend

High-yield stocks, dividend sustainability, income-oriented research.

Cyclical

Commodity cycles, low PE traps, resources, and capacity cycles.

Event-driven

Policy catalysts, restructuring, hot themes, and event risk.
